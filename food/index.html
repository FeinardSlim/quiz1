Food

My Favorite Food In Jakarta

Ketoprak Jakarta

This food, from Indonesia is similar to the famous "Gado-Gado". With the nut sauce, it consists of tofu, lontong (compressed rice cake), beehoon, boiled egg, bean sprouts and cabbage, doused in peanut sauce and topped with fried shallots and kerupuk (deep-fried flavoured rice crackers).

Kerak Telor

kerak Telor is one of the famous food in Jakarta. It is consist of egg, shredded coconut, and dried shrimp. As a Betawi tradition snack, kerak telor often served on a annual fair Jakarta Fair and sometimes in events. In the past, Kerak Telor was also considered to be food for the wealthy and served during parties.

Soto Betawi

Indonesia have alot of soto varians, and in Jakarta there is a famous soto. It called Soto Betawi. It consist of beef, coconut milk, and fresh milk, simmered with aromatic herbs and spices like galangal, garlic, shallots, kaffir lime leaves, and candlenut.

Nasi uduk Betawi

This is one of the most famous and common food in Jakarta, and even in whole Java. Nasi uduk can be served as breakfast, with a simple side dish of telur dadar gulung (rolled fried egg). It can also be served as lunch/dinner, typically with ayam ungkep (fried chicken), fried tempeh, fried tofu, and sambal terasi (shrimp paste chili sauce) on top of the egg. Ingredients: rice, coconut milk, lemongrass, pandan, ginger, galangal, Indonesian bay leaves, and coriander powder
